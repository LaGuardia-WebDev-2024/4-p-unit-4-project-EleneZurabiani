My Unofficial Local Guide to Avatar The Last Airbender

Ba Sing Se

There is no war in Ba Sing Se... It's a peacful city and the CAPITAL of the EARTH KINGDOM! How cool is that?!
It's a massive, ancient metropolis with a history of being founded underground and is structured in rings, much like Beijing
Let's go check it out!!



The Cave of Two Lovers

The cave is located deep within the Earth Kingdom, near the city of Omashu.
There is a "Secret Love Cave" altar which is a specific chamber within the cave where the story of the
two lovers took place and where a vert pretty heart-shaped rock is located.
We out or we out?


Omashu

Omashu is located in the Kolau Mountain Range in the southwestern Earth Kingdom, built on a mountain that rises high out of a deep gorge.
The Palace sits on the tallest hill, which is in the center behind some of the other hills.
The views are breathtaking and so worth visiting.
